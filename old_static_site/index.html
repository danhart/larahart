Welcome to my website. I am a Gestalt counsellor and psychosexual and relationship therapist, offering friendly, confidential and professional counselling in North London. I work in East Finchley N2 and Muswell Hill N10.

I have designed this website to help:
  • You consider how counselling might benefit you as an individual or as a couple
  • To give you a brief insight into how the gestalt approach works
  • To give you an idea of my work and experience; my professional qualifications and ongoing professional training.
  • Answer some of the questions you may have.

I also hope to provide you with the information you need to take the next step towards finding the best counsellor for you. I have included how to contact me, what takes place at an initial meeting and some links to other professional counselling sites if you would like further information or have questions.
